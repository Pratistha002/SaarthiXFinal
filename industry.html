Industry Services

Discover how SaarthiX can help your organization find the right talent, streamline your recruitment process, and engage with the academic community. Our industry services are designed to connect you with qualified candidates and future leaders.

Post Jobs

Publish your job openings on our platform to reach a wide pool of qualified candidates. Specify your requirements and let our matching algorithm find the best fits for your positions.

Post a Job

Database Access

Get access to our extensive database of pre-screened candidates with verified skills and qualifications. Search and filter based on your specific requirements and connect directly.
